## Design Principles 
- There are many FPL planning apps that exist today however these fail to engage users. They are often basic, and not visually appealing.
- The selling point of FPL Unlocked is that it's an FPL app for dedicated Premier League fans.
- The page and theme should resonate with both newer generations used to the current Premier League and older fans who grew up during the Barclays Premier League times. References to both themes [below](#themes)
- Users of the application should be able to choose the theme that they prefer and that theme should be persisted within the user's session.
- Option to select the Retro Theme (Barclays Premier League) or Modern Theme (Premier League).
